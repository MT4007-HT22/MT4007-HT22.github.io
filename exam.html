Electronic exam (1.5 hp, betyg A-F)

An electronic exam, 1.5 hp, is scheduled on 2023-01-05, 12:00-16:00. It will be a open book home exam and its allowed to use google. We reserve the right to conduct an oral exam after the exam, e.g., when we are correcting the exam. If you are subject to the oral exam, you will need to describe your code, what it does and why you chose to solve the question(s) the way you did. This will test your skills in using the tools introduced in the course and involves live coding in an RStudio environment with internet access. Here is some further information

  1. There will be four questions, at least one of them will be from the class questions listed below.
  2. The exam will be available here, on Github on the 05/01 12:00 which you will download (however you choose to).
  3. You hand in your exam answers in a .Rmd (code and accompanying text) through this link. If you have extended time for the exam (förlängd skrivningstid) then its this link.
